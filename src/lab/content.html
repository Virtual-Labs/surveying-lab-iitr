Introduction

Welcome to the Surveying Lab developed at IIT Roorkee. The interactive experiments in this lab offers students supplemental experience in fundamental land surveying measurement methods for surveying courses

List of experiments
  • Study of various parts of Auto Level
  • Carry out Fly Leveling using Auto Level
  • Profile Leveling using Auto Level
  • Observations of Vertical and Horizontal angles using Total Station
  • Study of Plane Table and its Accessories
  • Detail Plotting by Radiation Method
  • Detail Plotting by Intersection Method
  • Detail Plotting by Resection Method
  • Carry out Contouring in the field
  • Study of Global Positioning System (GPS) and its Accessories
  • Observations using GPS
Target Audience

The experiments are targeted at students who have just finished a first course in Civil Surveying.

Courses Aligned

The present lab is aligned with courses such as Surveying, and the like. The experiments touch on most topics covered in such courses in most curricula.

Prerequisite S/W

  • Java Runtime Environment allows you to play online virtual labs.
  • Quest3D Viewer
